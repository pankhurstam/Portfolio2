Our Social Media Mission

Through our WIU "Official News" Facebook Page and other social media platforms, our goal is to promote WIU, as well as provide the WIU community and those interested in WIU with a platform on which they can share content, ideas, and experiences, as well as ask questions and find helpful information. We try to provide accurate information and reasonable, transparent answers to questions and concerns.

Visit wiu.edu/brand to learn more about the WIU branding efforts with University Marketing.

Social Media Profile Templates

The page name on your social media platform(s) should appear in the following format:

Example #1: Western Illinois University RPTA or WIU Recreation, Park and Tourism Administration
Example #2: Western Illinois University CSP or WIU College Student Personnel
Example #3: Western Illinois University Biology or WIU Biology

Facebook

Facebook pages should consist of a profile photo, contact information, a short "about" bio to give viewers a better understanding of the page's content and a statement of the purpose of the page. A cover photo should be included, a photo suggested, and posts should be created regularly to communicate to students, promote WIU and the program, and showcase what goes on in the classroom. These pages are a great resource to provide students and parents with a behind-the-scenes look into the program running the page. Prepare content that is engaging, but also directed toward an adult audience.

Twitter

Twitter pages should consist of a profile photo, contact information, website link, a short "about" bio to give viewers a better understanding of the page's content and a statement of the purpose of the page. A cover photo should be included, a photo suggested, and tweets should be created regularly to communicate to students, promote WIU and the program, and showcase what goes on in the classroom. Twitter is a great resource to provide students and parents with a behind-the-scenes look into the program running the page. Content on Twitter is often criticized and can be retweeted quickly, therefore, should be censored critically.

Instagram

Instagram is a visually-based social platform in which looks truly matter. Create engaging content with clever, witty captions. One-line captions work best, but informational posts are also helpful as long as the caption is brief. The shorter, the better. Try to utilize photos as much as possible and let the photo tell the story of the mood, feelings you're trying to convey through the post.

Profile Photos

Academic Departments should utilize the W Logo. Clubs, Organizations, Community, and non-acadmic pages should utilize the Rocky Head Logo as the profile photo. While these logos are interchangeable, we encourage page managers to utilize the logo specific to their area to maximize extra social platform features and visuals. For questions about which image to use, please contact University Relations.
