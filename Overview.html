Overview


Are you looking to become a better content creator? Do you have a job that requires an update in skillset? This instructional website will help you to maximize your professional potential through a series of online tutorials, videos, and activities. After the completion of the website tutorials, you should expect to be able to convert videos for social media use, create graphics, engage with followers across social media platforms, and develop a cohesive social media appearance.


Before beginning instruction, you should know basic social media platforms and know what content creation is. Before working on the assignment, you will need to know how to utilize Adobe Creative Cloud products, how to operate a smartphone, and how to operate social media platforms and understand terminology associated. Resources are provided to help you through the instructional website and complete tutorials provided.


This website provides you with the opportunity to put virtual instructional content to use by creating your own video and graphics before publishing them to mobile apps like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.
