Welcome to the Content Creation Team!

You have either been recommended or volunteered to be a member of the WIU Campus Influencers team. You probably have BIG energy, are super creative, and love showing your WIU pride. The University Relations office has assembled this team to give students a chance to speak directly to other students and peers through awesome social media content! It's a great way to showcase your content creation skills while also gaining a few followers from TikTok, Instagram, or Snapchat tags! (And it’s a great resume booster!) The two platforms we're heavily focused on are TikTok and Instagram, so know that these platforms are where you'll more than likely see yourself!

Some examples of the kind of content we’re looking for includes:

  • TikTok video recreations
  • Campus timelapse videos
  • Interviews with students
  • Motivational videos
  • Resident hall room tours
  • Campus vlogs
  • A Day in the Life of a Leatherneck videos
  • WIU pride/WIU flag photos/video/GIFs
