Conclusion

This website serves as an instructional guide dedicated to teaching student content creators at WIU how to create videos, graphics, and content for social media platforms while adhering to brand guidelines. This website is designed for practical use in that it will be passed down to future creators as an on-job training module. Topics like social media platforms, the kind of content created for each, the sizes and ratios for content, instructional videos, examples, branding imagery, and links to official University branding guidelines are utilized within the website.

By the time you've reached this page, you should be able to:

  • Develop static graphics for social media platforms as measured by using the correct canvas size.

  • Recognize the social media platforms commonly utilized at WIU by completing a short quiz featured on the training website and achieving at least a 60%.

  • Develop horizontal videos from vertical videos in Adobe Premiere Pro as measured by the correct file type and frame size.

  • Identify correct branding elements as stated on the instructional website by completing a short embedded Google Form with correct answers.
